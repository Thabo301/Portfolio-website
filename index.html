My Journey

My tech career didn't start in a fancy office - it started in call centers and on-site with clients, untangling network cables and solving real-world problems. That hands-on experience at places like Clicks Retailers taught me how massive enterprise systems actually work and, more importantly, how they break. I realized I didn't just want to fix things; I wanted to build better, smarter systems from the ground up. This led me to a deliberate, self-funded pivot. I went all-in on mastering the cloud, not just one piece of it, but the whole 360-degree view. I wanted to understand how the infrastructure (IaaS), the data (PaaS), and the intelligence (AI) all connect. The projects you see on this site are the direct result of that journey.
